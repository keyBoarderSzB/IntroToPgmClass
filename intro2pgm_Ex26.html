Restaurant Review

The Village Smithy

Main Street
Carbondale, CO

The Review

Open only for breakfast and lunch, The Village Smith offers a wide variety of baked good and speciality creations. It is extremely popular and chances are you will be on a waiting list for a while unless you go really early morning.

Smithy Favorites

  • Scones, pies, and other freshly-baked delicacies
  • Unique omlette and egg creations
  • Fresh raspberry jam
